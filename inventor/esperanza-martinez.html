Mart√≠nez didn't just create technology and walk away. The results were transformative. Artisans could produce more embroidered pieces in less time, significantly increasing their incomes. Perhaps most importantly, the digital archiving aspect of Mart√≠nez's system preserved indigenous cultural heritage.
